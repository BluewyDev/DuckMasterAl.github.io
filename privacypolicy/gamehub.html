Game Hub Privacy Policy

By adding GameHub to your server, you agree to following Privacy Policy

What information is stored?

Before gamehub even joins your guild, your guild's id is added to our whitelist file.

When gamehub joins a guild, the guild's id is stored automatically into the gamehub database.

If you set an admin role, the role id will be stored.

When playing a game of Among Us, the voice channel id and every member in the voice channel's id is stored.

Why we store the information and how we use it.

We use the whitelist ids in order to make sure gamehub has permission to join your server.

On join, gamehub sets a category of our database with your guild id, so that you can use commands.

The admin role id is used to check if a user has the admin role or not.

The among us voice channel and voice channel members are used to mute/unmute users while playing a game of Among Us.

Who gets this data?

Only Developers can see the data the bot saves.

Questions and Concerns.

If you have questions/concerns about the data contact DuckMasterAl#0001 on Discord.

How to Remove your data.

Use the ",deletedata" command. This will delete your guild from our whitelist and the bot will leave your server.


Note: We reserve the right to change this without notifying our users.
