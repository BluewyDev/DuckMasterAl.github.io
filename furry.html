Am I a Furry?

Short Answer: No

Long Answer: I personally do not feel like I am a furry, and am not looking to be a furry. I am a duck.

Some people may classify me as a Furry due to my actions. For Example:

  • Saying uwu, owo, and the word boop
  • Having some fluff on my avatar

Even though these are traits that are used by furries, I personally do not identify as one, which is all that matters.

If you don't like that opinion and would like to keep calling me a furry, you will just be wrong and unaccepting of others.
