There are 3 different ways that you can discuss having Mental Health issues with.

  • You can go talk to a professional.
  • Talk to Therapy Channels in Discord.
  • Or you can talk to a close friend/family member!

The National Suicide Prevention Hotline isn't just for suicide, it can be used for talking to someone about your mental, physcial, and emotional needs.

We personally recommend going to them first, but do whatever you feel comfortable with.
